## Description:
Bakery's website containing information about the bakery and images of the different products. There is also a section where the user can leave a review for the bakery.

## User Stories
* One user will click the navbar to go down to leave a review for the bakery that they went to and they will input their email and use a range scale to rate their experience and then they will write in the text area about their experience.
* One user will click the gallery buttons to scroll through many images of the baked goods to decide what they will get. Then they will scroll down to the about us section to read more about the bakery.
* One user will click the delivery button in the navbar. They will input their address and email. They will choose from the list of baked goods to be delivered.
